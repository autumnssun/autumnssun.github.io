What is Soundit?

Soundit is an audio-based, inclusive game. Player starts the game by closing a sound to mimic, record, and send to a friend. The opponent then listen to the sound, guess what is the sound, and then send a sound back for his opponent.

The game can be played between visually impaired and non-visual impaired with equal chance of wining

The application
Game Concept

Inclusive

We want to make a game, not only fun to play among you and your friends. But people who blinded can also play, have fun, and have equal chance of winning the game with non-visual impaired people.

Social

Game is fun, especially when play with friends. Isn't it fun listen to your friends making wired noise, and trying to guess what that is. Visually impaired now can having fun playing game with their friends

Game activities

Listen

Your friend sent you a sound. He is making a sound of something. You will listen you that sound of him. You can listen to it as many time as you like.

Guess

After listen to the sound, you need to guess what it is. You have four trials. The less trial you use the more point you get. Don't worry, we have hint for you!

Reply

It is fun listen to your friend sound! Do you want him to listen to you? Send him back a sound, challenge him, and let him enjoy listen to you
